Government Schemes for Cancer Treatment

Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

A flagship health insurance program providing coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family annually for cancer treatments, benefiting over 68 lakh patients nationwide.

Learn More

Health Minister's CPF

Offers financial aid up to ₹15 lakh for cancer treatment at 27 Regional Cancer Centres, specifically for below poverty line patients.

Learn More

Haryana Stage III & IV Support

Monthly assistance of ₹3,000 for Stage III and IV cancer patients in Haryana with family income below ₹3 lakh annually.

Learn More

Arunachal Free Chemotherapy

Provides free chemotherapy up to ₹10 lakh yearly with consultation services, in partnership with TATA Memorial Hospital.

Learn More

UP CM Financial Assistance

State initiative providing financial aid through multiple channels including CM Helpline and Janata Darshan for cancer care support.

Learn More

MJPJAY Maharashtra

Healthcare scheme offering coverage up to ₹1.5 lakh annually for eligible ration card holders across Maharashtra's charitable hospitals.
