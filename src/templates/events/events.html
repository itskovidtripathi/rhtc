International Cancer Conferences

RHTC organizes forward-looking conferences across the globe to bridge implementation gaps in cancer care. These events bring together policymakers, healthcare professionals, researchers, and advocates to foster collaboration and address region-specific challenges.

Why it matters

These conferences drive regional solutions and global collaboration, integrating innovations like digital pathology and advanced diagnostics into healthcare systems.
